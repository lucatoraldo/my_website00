The early years

I was born in 1996 in Rome, Italy. There, I grew up in a typical Italian family, composed by my mom, my dad and my two younger sisters. I have always loved the thrill of exploring opportunities and taking the unconventional route. In high school I was the only student in my class to enroll in an exchange programme that took me to a small town outside New Orleans, USA, called Ponchatoula. Since I was a child I have always loved sports and I have had the honor of representing Italy at several international competitions, both as a swimmer and as a member of the Modern Pentathlon team.

University life and work experience

After I graduated from high school I decided to enroll to Bocconi University in Milan. Towards the end of my bachelor degree I received an offer from UBS, a prestigious investment bank, which prompted me to take a gap year and experience first hand the finance world. Few months later, as my internship in the Milan office of the Swiss bank was coming to an end, I was lucky enough to receive an offer for an off-cycle internship at Macquarie Capital in London. Currently, I am studying towards the Master in Financial Analysis at LBS, one of the most prestigious and renowned business schools in the globe.
